What is PBhere?

PBhere is a unfolding story about a person who wakes up in a padded cell with nothing but a cell phone with a few apps on it. He uses this phone to communicate with people via TikTok and Twitter. His videos also somehow end up on YouTube. 

Get caught up on the story and try and give them a hand to figure out what is going on.

(PBhere is an alternate reality game created by yatoimtop)
